Notes

  • When the actual number hands won by a player at the end of a round is more than the calls (predictions) made before that round, the difference will be a bonus. The bonus will be appended to the calls after a period (#calls.#bonus).
  • The bonus is worth one 1⁄th of an actual hand. When a player has bonus in total, it will count as 1 actual hand.
